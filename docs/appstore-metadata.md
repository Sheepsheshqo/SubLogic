## App Privacy — Nutrition Label

In App Store Connect → App Privacy → fill in as follows:

### Data Types Collected

**1. Purchases**
- Type: Purchase History
- Why: App Functionality (to validate and restore SubLogic Premium)
- Linked to identity: No
- Used for tracking: No

> This is from RevenueCat which processes Apple purchase receipts.

### Everything else → Data Not Collected
- No contact info
- No location
- No health or fitness
- No financial info (bank/card details)
- No identifiers (device ID, advertising ID)
- No usage data sent to us
- No diagnostics sent to us

## GitHub Pages Setup (to activate the Privacy Policy URL)

1. Push this repo to GitHub: `github.com/burhanbisgin/SubLogic`
2. Go to repo **Settings → Pages**
3. Source: **Deploy from a branch**
4. Branch: `main` / Folder: `/docs`
5. Click Save — URL will be: `https://burhanbisgin.github.io/SubLogic/`
6. Privacy policy will be live at: `https://burhanbisgin.github.io/SubLogic/privacy.html`
